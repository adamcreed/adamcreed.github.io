Titles

Hover over the spoiler bar after each title to show unlock condition. Unlock information from h1g.jp

  • ひとりたび (hitori tabi) - Travelling Alone
    • Go on your first trip
  • きままな旅行者 (kimamana ryokousha) - Carefree Traveler
    • Go on 10 trips
  • さすらいの旅人 (sasuraino tabibito) - Wandering Traverser
    • Go on 25 trips
  • はてなき冒険家 - (hatenaki bouken-ka) Eternal Adventurer
    • Go on 50 trips
  • めきき名人 (mekiki meijin) - Connoisseur
    • Received every kind of specialty foodstuff
  • おさんぽ日和 (osanpo biyori) - Nice Day for a Walk
    • Go on a trip and return in under 30 minutes
  • かえらぬ旅路 (kaeranu tabiji) - Endless Journey
    • Go on a trip and return in 1440 minutes or more
  • かえるにみつ葉 (kaeruni mitsuba) - Return to Clovers
    • Harvest 10,000 3-leaf clovers
  • かみだのみ (kamidanomi) - Prayer to the Gods
    • Play the lottery 20 times
  • お菓子なごはん (okashina gohan) - Candy Lunch
    • Take Konpeitou (the red ball prize from the lottery) on a trip four times in a row
  • まんじゅう怖い (manjuu kowai) - Dreadful Steamed Buns
    • Own 30 Hot Spring Steamed Buns/温泉まんじゅう. Click "Steamed Buns" above for a picture.
